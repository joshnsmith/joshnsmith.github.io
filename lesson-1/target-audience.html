Target Audience

The targetted group is mainly people from the area. There could also be site visitors from people traveling through or for an extended stay. The age would range anywhere from 18 + and income could be anywhere from $10,000/year or higher. The average person's eductional experience would be high school or above. Finally they could range from single, married or other.

Personas:

Fred: Fred decides he is tired of waiting for the nightly weather update from his favorite news channel. He goes online and searches on the internet: Weather Idaho. The first link that pops up is weatherittogether.com. He thinks the site has a friendly name and clicks on it. He then sees local weather, and even some news updates. He is excited about the website and what it will have to offer in the future.

Cali: Cali is a middle aged mother. She is constantly going outdoors and loves to explore the nature on her daily jogs. However some days her husband takes the car to work and she can't rely on the state wide weather site to provide her with the most accurate forecast's. She ask's her neighbor Sue and she recommends her to this site. She can now get the best news updates and never miss a day excersizing.

Scenarios

Sometimes its frustrating living in an area and not having a localized place to go for the weather. You always have to go to a bigger website and search you hometown then find the weather. Well complain no further. You can go to weatherittogether.com for localized weather updates in Small cities in Idaho.
