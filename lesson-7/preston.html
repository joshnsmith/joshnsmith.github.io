Winter Wonderland

This year we have seen a lot of snow fall. Every single year we get a lot of snowfall but this year in particular has been special. We have seen many things like this year before but one particular reason will make this year go down in history.

The amount of snowfall and crazy weather it has been has had everyone on their toes. With Covid-19 still impacting many lives in Idaho, the snow is also putting up a big impact. We have seen many inches of snow and may keep piling up day by day. However one good outcome is the kids! They love to play and have fun all geared up in their coats and hats.
